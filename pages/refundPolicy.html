Refund Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

30-day money-back guarantee

If Formulary isn't the right fit within 30 days of your first paid subscription, we'll refund your payment in full — no questions asked.

1. Free trial

Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required during the trial. If you decide not to subscribe, your account simply expires — there's nothing to cancel and nothing to refund.

2. Monthly subscriptions

If you cancel within 30 days of your first payment, we'll issue a full refund. After 30 days, cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle and no refund is issued for the remaining period.

You can cancel at any time from your account settings. No phone call or email required.

3. Annual subscriptions

Annual plans are billed upfront for the full year. If you cancel within 30 days of your annual payment, you receive a full refund. After 30 days, we refund the unused whole months remaining, prorated from the cancellation date.

Example: If you paid for 12 months and cancel after 4 months and 15 days, you'll be refunded for the remaining 7 full months.

4. How refunds are processed

  • Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–10 business days
  • You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is initiated
  • Bank processing times may add 2–5 additional business days

5. What happens to your data

After cancellation, your data remains available for export for 30 days. You can download your product catalogue, sales history, batch records, and customer data in CSV or JSON format. After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from our production systems.

6. Exceptions

We do not issue refunds for accounts terminated due to violation of our Terms & Conditions. If you believe your account was terminated in error, please contact us and we will review the case.

7. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, email us at hello@formulary.app. We aim to respond within one business day.
