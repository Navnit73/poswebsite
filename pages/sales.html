SERVER-SIDE VERIFICATION

Prices verified on every line item.

When a sale is submitted, the server re-calculates every line item from the database price, batch cost, and applicable tax rate. If the client-side total doesn't match, the sale is rejected. This prevents price manipulation at the register.

  • Atomic stock deduction — two cashiers can't oversell the same batch
  • Tax calculated per product based on HSN code
  • Thermal receipt and A4 invoice printing
