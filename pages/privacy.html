Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Who we are

Formulary Software ("Formulary", "we", "us") provides cloud-based inventory management and point-of-sale software for retail pharmacies. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you entrust to us when you use our website and application.

2. Data we collect

Account data: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and pharmacy name to create your tenant account.

Business data: Product catalogues, batch records, inventory movements, sales transactions, customer records, and staff profiles that you enter into the application. This data is scoped to your tenant and inaccessible to other accounts.

Usage data: We collect anonymized usage analytics (pages visited, features used, session duration) to improve the product. We do not track individual user behavior for advertising.

Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information logged for security and audit purposes.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and maintain the Formulary application
  • To send you transactional emails (login links, daily reports, alerts)
  • To improve our product based on anonymized usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • To detect and prevent security threats and abuse

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers who help us operate the application (cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing), and only to the extent necessary for them to perform their services. All service providers are bound by data processing agreements.

5. Data security

Your business data is isolated at the database layer using tenant IDs. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. All API endpoints are rate-limited and input is validated with Zod schemas. Sensitive fields (emails, passwords, tokens) are automatically redacted from audit logs. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

6. Data retention

Your business data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data remains available for export for 30 days. After that, it is permanently deleted from our production systems and backups within 90 days.

7. Your rights

  • Access: You can export all your data at any time from account settings.
  • Correction: You can edit your account and business data directly in the application.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us.
  • Portability: Your data can be exported in CSV and JSON formats.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We use anonymized analytics cookies to understand how the product is used. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to the account owner at least 14 days before they take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at hello@formulary.app or visit our contact page.
